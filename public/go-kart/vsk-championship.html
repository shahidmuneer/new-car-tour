ARE YOU READY TO RACE?

Do you want to feel the real thrill of a go-kart competition? This is the place for you! VEGAS SUPERKARTS organizes the VSK RACING SERIES, a sprint and endurance go-kart championship open to every level of driver, with race events happening every last Sunday of the month. Join us for some fun yet challenging races and score points throughout the year for the championship!

On top of that, your points will be added to the Sodi World Series (SWS) with a chance to qualify for the World finals in Dubai! In order to register for the races, create your profile on the SWS website and you will be automatically added to their ranking.

Every race event will be concluded with a podium ceremony along with trophies for the top three racers. Join us now for some thrilling races as seats are limited for each event!

ALL DRIVERS MUST BE REGISTERED FOR BOTH RACES UNTIL TUESDAY BEFORE THE EVENT!

INFORMATION
Age Driver must be 15 years old to race in the VSK SPRINT RACING SERIES and 10 years old to race in the VSK JUNIOR RACE.
Equipment Vegas Superkarts provides helmets and balaclavas as part of the rental experience to ensure you are fully immersed with a high level of safety.
Driver Weight Ballast may be used for drivers weighing under 175lbs. Base weight is taken without gear.
Kart Assigment Drivers will pick their karts at random before going on track.
Registration Register here until the Tuesday before the event. BE CAREFUL - ALL DRIVERS MUST BE REGISTERED FOR BOTH RACES.
Prices & Fees Full payment must be made by credit card to Exotics Racing (702-405-7223) by the Tuesday before the race event. Unpaid drivers will be removed from the race registration. There are no other additional hidden fees, day licenses or automatic memberships required to race.

What is Sodi World Series1?

Sodi World Series is an international ranking of the top Sodi rental kart drivers in the world. It’s the biggest worldwide go-karts ranking system and is only available at VEGAS SUPERKARTS in Las Vegas. Drive & win points race after race to work your way up in the championship ranks.

Check your performances on the SWS website through personalized statistics which allow you to compare yourself with your friends & drivers from all over the world.

Open to everyone, the SWS is a fantastic way of enjoying karting differently, and for the speediest of you, to test your driving skills in the Sodi World Finals!

Subscribe for free, choose your racing category & put the pedal to the metal!
