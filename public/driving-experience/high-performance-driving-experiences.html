1-Day Driving Experience

Becoming an octane addict starts with Exotic Racing’s 1-Day Driving Experience. It is a perfect way to introduce yourself to the thrills of high-speed driving and be guided by a skilled professional who will help you unlock your potential.

All of our programs are designed around one-to-one instruction techniques. First, you will start with Exotic Racing professional driver taking you for dynamic drive and setting an example of achieving the best performance and taking the ideal line through the curves. Then, you will take the driver’s place and show your skills. After two discovery laps, you will get a multi-lap session with the instructor and post-session in which you will analyze your performance with data debrief. The video analysis is included, and you will be amazed how much you will learn and how your skills will be improved after our 1-Day Driving Experience. You will emerge as a much better, safer, and confident driver after a stint in Corvette C8 Z51, Porsche 911 GT3 RS, Ferrari 488 Challenge EVO, or McLaren GT4

YOUR EXPERIENCE

Sharpen your skills with a private instructor.

Designed to improve your ability behind the wheel, all four of our programs begin with the key skills needed to improve lap times, including braking and shifting techniques and car control dynamics. Off-track, our programs use a high-level driving simulator, essential for building muscle memory and track knowledge where you find the limits without consequence. We also use the innovative Batak reflex game just as professional racing drivers around the world do to test and improve your hand-eye coordination and reaction times.



ACTIVITIES

Private Briefing

The interactive technical briefing will introduce you to our track and proper driving procedures that will make you a more precise and faster driver. Our team will ensure that you feel comfortable and prepared before you get behind the wheel!

Two Discovery Laps

Ride along with one of our professional drivers behind the wheel in order to get a preview of our racetrack. The Discovery Laps are driven at moderate speed in order to explain each braking zone, apex, and acceleration zone.

3-Lap Ride-Along

Observe the expertise of a professional driver on this 3-lap ride-along! Learn how they navigate the track and observe their braking and shifting techniques in order to improve your own lap times.

On-Board HD Video & Data Analysis

Recorded with a Performance Data Recorder, you will have a high-definition video of your driving followed by an in-depth meeting with your coach to review the data. You will have the chance to discuss and analyze how you can improve the next time around!

1 Go-Kart Race

Hop into one of our amazing 4-stroke gas engine go-karts and enjoy a race on a sensational purpose-built racetrack! Test your skills and push yourself to the limit in some wheel to wheel fun.

Shop Tour

Take a peek behind the scenes and see where our supercars get ready for the track! You will have a chance to tour our shop where you will see our talented mechanics working in action on our fleet.

Batak

The Batak challenge is a game specifically designed to improve your hand eye co-ordination, reaction time, and stamina while also providing an element of fun and competition. Measure how your reaction speed compares to those of professional Formula 1 drivers! It is as simple as pressing the button when it lights up as quickly as you can before moving onto the next one.
