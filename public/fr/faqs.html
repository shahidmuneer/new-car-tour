Frequently Asked Questions

Below you will find answers to the most frequently asked questions about Exotics Racing. If you do not find the answers you are looking for please contact one of our friendly customer service representatives at 702-405-7223.



Racetrack Experience

What can I expect when I get to the track?

After checking in, all drivers will attend a welcome briefing followed by a technical instruction giving you a better idea of how to tackle our track. Afterward, drivers will discover our track in person, riding along in a Porsche SUV, driven by our lead instructor. Our instructor will demonstrate the racing techniques you've just learned during the technical instruction. This will help you feel comfortable on your very first lap. Participants are then introduced to their personal driving instructor who will sit in the passenger seat with them during their driving experience. The instructor ensures that you take full advantage of the car, while operating in a safe environment. After your driving experience you will receive a certificate of completion. On-board video, Drifting Ride-Alongs, additional laps, additional cars are available onsite as supplements.

What are your operating hours?

For Exotics Racing , we are open 6 days a week, closed on Wednesdays from 9am-4pm. For Vegas Superkarts, we are open 7 days a week 10am-5pm.

Do you provide transportation services?

We do not offer transportation.

If you do not have a means of transportation to and from our track, we recommend taking an Uber, Lyft, or taxi to our location if you do not have your own car for arrival. Prices start at $40-$60 one-way. 

Presidential Limo by KAPTYN offers private door to door transportation to EXR. We recommend scheduling pick up at least 30 minutes prior to your track time directly from the front door of the property of your choice. KAPTYN will then return you to any property of your choice after your experience. Please call 702-438-5466 to book. 

What should I wear?

Participants need to wear comfortable clothes and closed-toe shoes, preferably sneakers. Helmets are mandatory and provided free of charge. Sandals and high-heels are not acceptable. 

What is the technical briefing all about?

No matter what level of driving experience you have, we require you to attend the technical briefing. In this briefing our professional instructors will explain the following information in detail.
- Proper driving position
- Steering techniques
- Car specifications (power, gearbox, braking, and most importantly, accelerating).
- Proper racing lines

Are helmets required?

 Yes, all participants are required to wear helmets while in the car, and on track. Exotics Racing provides Helmets at no charge for all participants.

Can I come with friends and family?

Yes, your friends and family are welcome to attend and will be able to take videos and pictures from our secure viewing areas. Children are welcome, however, for safety reasons we do not recommend children under the age of 24 months attend this experience. Pets are not allowed our facilities.

Can my guest ride with me in the car?

No, only a driver and instructor will be in the car. There is an opportunity for guests to accompany the participant when they go for their discovery laps. The cost is $20 per additional person.

Can I use my own camera to record inside the car?

Because of our insurance policy, we do not allow any other camera or video equipment in the cars. We do offer an on-board video of your driving experience that you may purchase prior to driving.

How long should I plan for the whole experience?

 Plan for a minimum of 2 hours. Plan for half a day if you are driving for more than 15 laps.

Can I upgrade my experience?

Depending on availability, you may be able to change which car you drive or drive additional cars. You can also buy additional laps, choose add-ons such as a video of your driving experience or buy a Drifting Ride-Along Experience in our Dodge Hellcat SRT.

Can I drive more than 1 car?

Yes, we offer the ability to book combo packages. These combos are designed for one driver and not to be split up with multiple drivers. The combo package helps provide you with the opportunity to compare and contrast power and handling of these exciting supercars.

Can we buy food and drinks on the track?

Yes. Snacks and beverages available for purchase. Alcohol is not permitted at our track facility.

What happens when it rains?

All our cars are equipped with all the proper security systems and tires to handle driving in any condition. In extreme cases where the weather forces us to close the track, we can reschedule you at any of our tracks, free of charge.

How to contact us?

 You may reach us at 702 405 7223 or you can e-mail us at experience@exoticsracing.com

Can I bring my drone to film on your racetrack?

Unfortunately, no drones are allowed on track due regulations placed by Nellis Airforce Base.

Can the multiple car package be split amongst drivers?

All our packages and multiple car packages are per driver and cannot be split amongst multiple drivers.

How to buy a gift certificate?

You can purchase an EXR Gift Certificate online at our website www.exoticsracing.com . Or you can purchase an EXR Gift Certificate over the phone by calling us at (702) 405-7223, Mon-Fri 7AM to 7PM, Sat-Sun 8AM-6PM, and Holidays 8AM-6PM

How can I pay?

There are 4 payment options:
1. Credit Card: Visa, MasterCard, Discover, and American Express are accepted.

2. PayPal: Website only/ 

3. Check: Order is only confirmed after the full payment is processed.
4. Cash: This is only possible on the track. However, we cannot guarantee availability if the reservation is not paid in advance.

Can I pay in installments?

No, the reservation must be paid in full prior to your reservation date.

Can I pay today and book a date later?

Yes, you can purchase an EXR Gift Certificate, which is valid until redeemed for use. With an EXR Gift Certificate, you or the recipient, can book a driving experience on our website or by phone at (702) 405-7223.

I can't decide on the car to choose, can I just buy an open gift certificate?

Yes, you can purchases an EXR Gift Certificate, which is valid until redeemed for use. With an EXR Gift Certificate, you or the recipient, can book a driving experience on our website or by phone at (702) 405-7223.

I need a present today! Can I book a driving experience for someone last minute?

Yes, depending on availability. You can also purchases an EXR Gift Certificate and print it immediately. You can also add a personal message to it before printing!

Can I change the recipient of my Gift Certificate?

Yes, it is fully transferable to another recipient of your choice. Please contact us with the name of the new recipient so we can update the reservation. Please note that it does not change expiration date of the gift certificate.

Can I change the Experience I have selected?

 Yes, depending on availability. If there is a price difference, you will be responsible to pay that difference before a change to the reservation can be made.

How do I book a date for the experience?

We are currently offering more than 260 dates each year with a variety of session times per track day. Please choose a date from our online schedule, you can see all our open dates here: Exotics Racing Track Schedule. If you have questions or wish to book with a live person you can reach our customer service representatives at: (702) 405-7223. For large groups of 10 people or more we may be able to open a date that is not currently offered. If you have a potential group event please contact a Group Sales Representative here: Exotics Racing Group Events.

How many days ahead do I need to make a reservation?

Depending on the period, the waiting time can be from 1 day, 2 weeks, or 3 months. For that reason, we strongly recommend that you book your experience as soon as possible. If you have an EXR Gift Certificate, please book your driving experience by contacting our customer service representatives for a last minute booking at (702) 405-7223.

Help! I did not receive my confirmation letter.

Check your email's spam folder to ensure it was not missed. You can also check your order details online here:www.exoticsracing.com/members/login-to-your-account.htm
If you have any problems accessing your account, please call us (702 405-7223) and we can re-send your order confirmation. You should also receive a reminder email prior to your event date with the order details.

Do gift certificates expire?

 Gift certificates are valid for up to 5 years from the purchase date. Please contact our customer sales representative at (702) 405 7223 to schedule a date and time for your experience.

Can I modify my schedule or ask for a refund?

 Reservations are not refundable. Your experience is valid up to 5 years from the purchase date until redeemed for use and can be rescheduled. Rescheduling fees apply for Track Buyouts, Mini Grand Prix, and Grand Prix experiences.

I was not able participate in the experience on the scheduled date, what can I do?

Please contact our customer sales representative at 702.405.7223 as soon as possible to reschedule your experience or to leave the experience open dated for a future date.

Is there a weight or height restriction?

Height and weight restrictions are dependent on build and we want to make sure that our customers know that the interior space of our exotic vehicles are smaller than inside most cars. The roomiest cars are the Porsche's, Audi R8, Nissan GT-R, and Ferrari's more so than Lamborghini's. As long as you feel comfortable in the exotic, we will be happy to accommodate. If you do not feel comfortable in the exotic that you reserve, our staff on track will gladly assist in choosing another exotic. Please keep in mind that it will be subject to availability.

What is the minimum age to participate?

You must be 18 years of age and have a valid drivers license to drive.
You must be 11 years, a minimum of  80 lbs and 4 ft 9 in or older to participate in the Dodge Hellcat SRT Drift Ride-Along, High-Speed Ride Along, and Discovery Laps.

Is insurance included?

Yes, insurance is included in your package. However, you and your guests will have to sign a liability waiver when you arrive at the track.

Deposit required?

 No deposit is required for your driving experience at Exotics Racing, however the reservation must be paid in full prior to the date of the experience.

Is an international drivers license acceptable?

 Yes. A valid drivers license issued by a government agency is acceptable.

Do I need to know how to drive a manual transmission?

No, all of our cars are equipped with paddle shifters. No need to worry if you don't know how to operate a manual transmission as there is no clutch to manually operate. All our supercars are easy for any driver to operate.

Do I need any prior racing experience?

Absolutely not! Our driving experiences are open to anyone. It doesn't matter what level of experience you have behind the wheel to drive our cars. Feel comfortable choosing 5, 10, or even a 30 lap experience. Just relax, have fun, and enjoy your drive.

I am a professional race car driver, can I drive alone?

 No, but we will make sure our professional driving instructors are aware of the fact so they give you more flexibility on the track.

Do you offer Military Discounts?

Yes, all military - veteran or active can receive a free lap on their driving experience with proof of military ID.

What Is GoKart?

What is included in the GoKart experience?

GoKart experiences include a helmet (required), neck protection, gloves, and headsock (optional).

How many laps are each race?

Each race is 8 minutes timed. Average drivers are able to get in 12-14 laps in this time.

Can I bring my own helmet?

Customers can bring their own close-faced helmet with approval at registration. Visors are required and must be used.

What should I wear?

We recommend comfortable clothing and require closed toed shoes. Scarves cannot be worn when racing GoKarts.

Should I tie my hair for the experience?

Long hair must be tied back.

Do you offer hand control?

We do not offer hand controls for our GoKarts.

Can we buy food and drinks on the track?

Yes. There is a cafe with burgers, salads, french fries, snacks, fruits, cookies and beverages available for purchase. This service is provided by Levy Restaurant. Alcohol is not permitted at our track facility.

How to contact us?

You may reach us at 702 802 5690 or you can e-mail us HERE

What is your rescheduling policy?

You are welcome to reschedule at any time. Simply call us at 702.802.5690 for assistance.

What is your refund / cancelation policy?

We have a no refund policy. All sales are final. Customers are welcome to reschedule their experience to another date and time.

Do I need any prior go-kart experience?

Absolutely not! Our driving experiences are open to anyone. It doesn't matter what level of experience you have behind the wheel of a go-kart. Just relax, have fun, and enjoy your drive.

I was not able participate in the experience on the scheduled date, what can I do?

Please contact our customer sales representative at 702.802.5690 as soon as possible to reschedule your experience or to leave the experience open dated for a future date.

What are the requirements to drive?

All 1-Seater GoKart participants must be a minimum of 14 years old and at least 55. Drivers of our 2-Seater GoKarts must be at least 16 years or older to have an accompanying guest. Guests must be at least 42 tall. All minors must have a legal guardian sign the waiver.
