1-Day Driving Experience Corvette C8 Z51

Once you’ve driven a supercar on a racetrack, you’ll understand the thrill of its potential. But what about your potential as a driver? That’s where Exotic Racing’s 1-Day Driving Experience comes in.

Our program is designed around one-on-one instruction. Ride-along laps with an Exotics Racing pro driver offer a dynamic example to follow and a target to reach. Then, it’s your turn. After 2 discovery laps, you’ll get three 8-lap sessions with instruction and post session data debriefs, including video analysis, to put the skills you’ve learned into practice. At the end of the day, you’ll emerge a more confident, better and faster high-performance driver.

Designed to improve your ability behind the wheel, our program begins with the key skills needed to improve lap times, including braking and shifting techniques and car control dynamics. Off-track, our program uses a high-level driving simulator, essential for building muscle memory and track knowledge where you find the limits without consequence.
