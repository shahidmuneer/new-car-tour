THE FERRARI 488 PISTA IS SO MUCH FASTER!
Pista is Italian for racetrack, and the 488 Pista was made for it.
FERRARI 488 PISTA. THE NAME SAYS IT ALL.
211 MPH. 2.8 sec 0-62 MPH. What are you waiting for? Get behind the wheel of this machine and add your lap time to the Michelin Time Trial Challenge!

Drive a Ferrari 488 Pista On a Racetrack!

“Enjoy the sonorous wail of the latest Ferrari: the 488 GTB”.

The Ferrari 488 Pista again raises the bar for what Ferrari can do to create simply brilliant cars. When you consider that they started from an already brilliant car in the 488 GTB and made the 488 Pista more powerful, lighter and ultimately faster should be more than enough to stop you in your tracks and do whatever it takes to get behind the wheel. read more >

It has the most powerful V8 engine that Ferrari has ever made, capable of 720 horsepower. It’s 200 pounds lighter than the 488 GTB, and has aerodynamics drawn directly from Ferrari racecars that won in Formula 1 and at Le Mans. It has driver aids that aren’t intended to hold you back, but instead to set you free to go faster. In fact it will go from a standstill to 62 MPH in 2.8 seconds and won’t quit until it reaches 211 MPH. The fastest time on the Michelin Time Trial scoreboard will likely to come under serious threat by the 488 Pista.

Ferrari’s legacy of greatness and desirability has only been strengthened by the 488 Pista. And that saying a lot.

Our Exclusive Racetrack

SUPERCAR DRIVING EXPERIENCES
Our Las Vegas location is at SpeedVegas Motorsports Park, just 15 minutes from the world famous Las Vegas Boulevard. Our exclusive 1.3-mile racetrack was designed by professional racing drivers to highlight our supercars performance capabilities. You'll find an example of many turns found on the world's greatest racetracks including hairpins, double apex corners, fast sweepers, chicanes, 90-degree corners and even a banked turns as well as large runoff areas, the latest high-tech safety barriers, and even the only in-ground LED lighting system in the US.
