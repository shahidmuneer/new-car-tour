1-DAY OFF-ROAD DRIVING EXPERIENCE

Once you’ve driven our Baja Race Truck on a race track, you’ll understand the thrill of its potential. But what about your potential as a driver? That’s where our 1-Day Driving Experience comes in.

Our program is designed around one-on-one instruction. Ride-along laps with an VEGAS OFF-ROAD EXPERIENCE pro driver offer a dynamic example to follow and a target to reach. Then, it’s your turn. After 2 discovery laps, you’ll get several multi-lap sessions with instruction and post session debriefs, including video analysis, to put the skills you’ve learned into practice. You will learn to understand weight transfer, throttle management, adapt to understeer and oversteer, induce and correct slides, drive the proper off-road line, braking technics, how to maximize traction and learn jumping technics. At the end of the day, you’ll emerge a more confident, better and faster off-road driver.

Arrival & Check-In

We advise you to arrive 30 minutes before the start of your experience, with a valid driver’s license. Without it, you won’t be able to drive.

Briefing

The interactive technical briefing will introduce you to our track and proper driving procedures that will make you a more precise and faster driver. Our team will ensure that you feel comfortable and prepared before you get behind the wheel!

Two Discovery Laps

Ride along with one of our professional drivers behind the wheel in order to get a preview of our track. The Discovery Laps are driven at moderate speed in order to explain each braking zone, apex, and acceleration zone.

Driving Experience

Get behind the wheel of our Baja Race Truck for three sessions, each 8 laps! Your personal instructor will help you get the absolute most out of your driving experience.

Data & Video Analysis

Recorded with a Performance Data Recorder, you will have a high-definition video of your driving followed by an in-depth meeting with your coach to review the data. You will have the chance to discuss and analyze how you can improve the next time around!

3-Lap Ride-Along

Observe the expertise of a professional driver on this 3-lap ride-along! Learn how they navigate the track and observe their braking and shifting techniques in order to improve your own lap times.

INFORMATION
Age Driver must be at least 18 years old with a valid driver’s license.
Equipment Vegas Off-Road Experience provides helmets, HANS systems and balaclavas to ensure you are fully immersed with a high level of safety.

Insurance A waiver form must be signed by the driver(s) at the time of check-in. The damage protection is available to purchase.
